Event Video Production

Every event has its own feeling, the people, the energy, the stories. At Munjiri Videos, we’re here to capture it all in a real, natural way. Whether it’s a big event, a wedding, a brand launch, or a small gathering, we’ll film the moments that matter most so you can share the memories and the feeling long after the day is done.

Why Event Video Production Matters

Event Video Production is one of the best ways to relive the energy and emotion of an event. Whether you’re hosting a product launch, or a personal milestone like a wedding, capturing the essence of the day through video makes sure that no moment is forgotten.
At Munjiri Videos, we specialize in turning your event into a cinematic story, preserving the atmosphere, connections, and details that make it unforgettable. Our videos not only highlight key moments but also convey the experience, emotions, and excitement that resonate with your audience.

Check out a video we made for Computer Aid to showcase the opening of their new solar power computer labs. 

What’s Included in Our Event Video Production Service

Before the big day, we’ll chat with you to figure out what you want your video to capture, whether it’s a feeling, a message, or both. We’ll make sure your video has a clear purpose, whether you’re planning to promote something, share it with your guests, or just keep the memories alive.

Everything You Need, Start to Finish

We’ve got the whole process covered:
A pre-event call to hear what you’re hoping for
Help with scripting or event planning if you need it
Professional filming with top-notch equipment
Multiple camera angles (and drone shots if it fits!)
Filming every key moment on the day
Editing magic: music, sound, color, and finishing touches
Final videos just the way you want: full recap, highlights, or even a livestream

Videos That Fit Your Event

No matter what you’re planning, we’ll make a video that feels right for your event:
Corporate events: launches, conferences, team get-togethers
Social gatherings: weddings, birthdays, family celebrations
Creative & community events: festivals, fundraisers, exhibitions, live shows
Brand activations: pop-ups, influencer events, product demos
Every event is different, and we make sure your video captures exactly what makes yours special.


Our Approach to Event Video Production

At Munjiri Videos, we’re all about capturing your event in a way that feels real and meaningful. We don’t just focus on the big moments, we catch the little ones too, the ones that really make the day special. Our team stays in the background, rolls with whatever comes up, and makes sure you can focus on enjoying the event.

Professional, but low-key: We work quietly and efficiently, so we’re never in the way or distracting from what’s happening.
Team players: We work closely with you before and during the event to make sure we’re on the same page about what matters most to you.
Quality: We use professional gear, cameras, drones, gimbals, you name it, to get beautiful footage from every angle.
